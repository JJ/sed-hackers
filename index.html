The talk that, since May 2013, I give to first-year students at the University of Granada ‚Äî and also in February 2016 at the University of Alicante. It is intentionally not recorded, so that I can speak honestly, sharing some inconvenient truths that they need to hear, and in order to invite the students to come forward and speak up, sharing their experiences in college.
